Social Media

Sometimes the fastest way to find out what's going on is to check our socials:

Facebook

  • Josephine Butler College JCR Facebook Group - a place where higher volumes of content sometimes gets shared, including photos to formals.

Instagram

  • @butlerjcr - for the main JCR
  • @butler.sabbs - for the President & FACSO
  • @teambutlersport - showcasing our sports teams
  • @butlersocieties - showcasing our societies

  • @josephine_butler_freshers_2025 - the dedicated page for all freshers

  • @butler_welfare - the page for our welfare team, who are here to offer support, resources, or a simple chat.
