Social Media

Sometimes the fastest way to find out what's going on is to check our socials:

Facebook

  • Josephine Butler College JCR Facebook Group - a place where higher volumes of content sometimes gets shared, including photos to formals.

Instagram

  • @butlerjcr - for the main JCR
  • @butler.sabbs - for the President & FACSO
  • @teambutlersport - showcasing our sports teams
  • @butlersocieties - showcasing our societies
  • @butler.vp - our vice president, who is responsible for college families, committees, and more
  • @josephine_butler_freshers_2025 - the dedicated page for all freshers
  • @jbcsummerball - our summer ball, which happens in June every year.
  • @jbwinterball - our winter ball, which happens in December every year.
  • @butlertechcomm - the tech committee, who deal with light, sound, and anything in between, for events
  • @butler_internationalcomm - the international committee, who help international students settle in, and promote cultural cohesion
  • @butler_welfare - the page for our welfare team, who are here to offer support, resources, or a simple chat.
  • @butler__edi - (note the double underscores!) the page for our EDI team, who are here to promote inclusion.
  • @butlercollege_principal - our college principal, Gill.
  • @pepper_borderterrier - our college principal's dog, Pepper.
