Our College Events

Butler Day

Butler Day is an event that happens after the exam season, and happens in June. In the past we have had many things, from dodgems to inflatables, food trucks to live performances!

Winter Ball

The winter ball happens towards the end of Michaelmas term, i.e. in December. You can come on a meals ticket, or an ents ticket (ents = entertainment).

Summer Ball

The summer ball happens towards the end of Easter term, i.e. in June. Like winter ball, you can come on a meals ticket, or an ents ticket (ents = entertainment), however unlike Winter Ball, it ends at 6am the following day (as a "survivors" ball), with a view of the sunrise on Observatory Hill!

Charity Fashion Show

The charity fashion show, as self-described, is a fashion show which happens every year, in order to raise money for charities. The charity fashion show usually includes the show itself, as well as other fundraising events. In the past, this includes a club night, and a fun run.
